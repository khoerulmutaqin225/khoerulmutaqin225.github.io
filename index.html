Fire and Motion Detector With Whatsapp

The purpose of this study is to design an Arduino-based fire and motion detector that can be accessed via a smartphone application, using Telegram and WhatsApp for communication. This study follows the R&D method which includes hardware and software design, as well as tool testing. This tool integrates sensors to detect fire and motion and an IP Camera for visual monitoring. Users simply connect the device to the Telegram and WhatsApp applications, then enter the username and password provided. This tool automatically sends notifications and images if fire or motion is detected, allowing real-time monitoring. The results show that the tool functions well, detects fire and motion accurately and sends information effectively via messaging applications.

IoT-Based Water Cooling Practicum

The purpose of this research is to design an instrument for physics practicum, namely air cooling that can be accessed by the practitioner from home by simply opening the smartphone application. This research is an R&D research through three stages, namely: hardware design, software design and tool testing. The development of this air cooling practicum tool consists of two parts, hardware in the form of practicum tools which will later be placed in the laboratory and software in the form of a blynk application on a smartphone that will be used to conduct practicum remotely. Practicum is done with the instruments that have been made. The practitioner only needs the blynk application, then enter the username and password. As a result, it is known that the development of the tool has been carried out, the tool has worked well, namely it can control the tools in the laboratory, turn on and turn off the electric stove, can display the tools in the laboratory because there is an IP Camera and data on the increase and decrease in air temperature can also be displayed on the blynk application on smartphone.

Appointment Jitsi

The Jitsi Appointment module brings seamless integration with the Jitsi API, allowing users to schedule, manage, and start meetings directly from within the app. With this capability, users can easily set the meeting time and date, and receive a Jitsi link to join the meeting with just one click. The module also stores meeting details and manages meeting history, while providing automated notifications and reminders to ensure that nothing gets missed. This integration ensures a connected and efficient meeting experience, without the need to switch to another platform.
